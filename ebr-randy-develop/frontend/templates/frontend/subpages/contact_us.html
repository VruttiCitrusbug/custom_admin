Contact Us

We like happy riders! If you’ve got a question about which ebike is the perfect fit, visit our help forum where moderators, dealers and other electric bike owners will be happy to assist. Once you’ve narrowed it down a bit, use the compare tool then search for the closest dealer using our directory map. They will be happy to answer your questions and guide you. We have also created a list of starter guides here including topics like electric bike vs. regular bike and how to choose an electric bike amongst others. We created these resources to help you because we can’t always respond to emails when we travel. Please note that we do NOT sell electric bikes, electric bike components, accessories, or any physical products. If you are attempting to purchase an ebike or to get support for one you already own, please contact the manufacturer directly as we cannot help you with that. If you have a business request please use the contact form below and we’ll get back to you at our earliest convenience! Most of the content on this website is created by Court Rye who is the founder of EBR and primary writer/videographer, I just want to say thanks for visiting and making this website such a great community.
